About This App

🌿 Purpose

This app provides awareness about traditional herbal remedies for common health problems. It helps rural communities make informed decisions about when to use home remedies and when to seek professional medical help.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

This app is for health awareness purposes ONLY. It does NOT replace qualified medical professionals, doctors, or hospitals. The information provided is based on traditional knowledge and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a doctor for proper medical care.
